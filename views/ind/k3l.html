Safety, Health and Environment

Safety, Health, and Environment (S.H.E) is the main priority of Inti Lingga Group and its subsidiaries in daily operations. S.H.E Department was established to create a safe, healthy and environmentally friendly working environment

We regularly conducts refreshment training to ensure that the company's S.H.E. slogan, “Safety is Our Way of Life”, is completely understood and implemented in our daily activities
