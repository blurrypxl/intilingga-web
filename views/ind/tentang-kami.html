About us

Inti Lingga was established on 1966 mainly as Shell's B2C lubricant distributor for the Jabodetabek region and since then has grown to become one of Indonesia's largest private sector distributor of commercial fuel and Shell lubricant. Amid the deregulation of the commercial fuel business on 1st of October 2005. The founder Drh. Julius Stiawan Jo had the vision to enter the commercial fuel business. Inti Lingga Group is now operating commercial fuel business for the Java Island, Southern Sumatera, and East Kalimantan.

Our vision

Become a leading distribution company in Indonesia with the excellent service to achieve customer satisfaction

Our mission

  • Expanding the distribution network Nationwide with consistency high quality of services.
  • Compiliance of standart industry practice and team work to distribute fuels product with integrated supply chain, effective, ontime delivery, and competitive price.
