Petroleum Trading and Distribution

We are a company engaged in the trade and distribution of petroleum, with a focus on providing high-quality diesel fuel to various industrial sectors. We understand the importance of stable and reliable energy in supporting smooth business operations, which is why we are committed to delivering fast, safe, and efficient services.

In our diesel fuel distribution efforts, we have an extensive network and solid infrastructure to ensure timely supply to various regions. Whether for industrial, transportation, construction, or other sectors, we provide fuel that meets international quality standards and is fully certified, ready to support the productivity of your business.

Quality Control For Every Products

At PT. Inti Lingga Sejahtera, we prioritize quality control to ensure that every diesel fuel product we offer meets the highest standards. Our quality control process includes Laboratory Testing, Visual Inspection, Environmental Standards, and Regular Audits.

Our Products

We offer a range of high-quality and certified fuels (HSD, MFO, and MDF) from LeMigas, designed to meet your industrial needs. Additionally, we provide high-quality lubricants (engine oil) to support your operational requirements.

Our Other Line of Business

PT Inti Lingga Sejahtera provides only high-quality fuel, and we are committed to offering comprehensive solutions that support every aspect of your business operations. In addition to our core fuel services, we proudly extend our expertise to two other key areas: lubricants and ship rental.
